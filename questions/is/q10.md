## English translation

In the current context, organizations are pressured to adopt increasingly resilient, responsive, and innovative positions. To do so, they must make efforts to assimilate environmental stimuli, whether from physical or virtual markets, evaluate their potential and limitations, and propose value that meets the expectations of their clients.

The following chart presents the main benefits for companies regarding the use of social networks.

Considering the information presented, evaluate the following statements.

I. Social networks represent opportunities for the area of organizational and competitive intelligence, requiring a new information architecture within organizations.  
II. The dynamic interaction of users through social networks provides input not only from consumers, but also from collaborators, and can be used for analysis and decision-making.  
III. The adoption of social networks by companies demonstrates their technical capacity to adopt these data in strategic planning and information architecture.  
IV. Organizational and competitive intelligence is based on the capacity of organizations to monitor and analyze information so that they can adapt and develop sustainably.

It is correct only what is stated in:

A. I and IV.  
B. II and III.  
C. III and IV.  
D. I, II, and III.  
E. I, II, and IV.
