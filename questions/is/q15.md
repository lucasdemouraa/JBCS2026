# ENADE 2021 Information Systems - Question 15

## Original question image

![Question 15](q15.png)

## English translation

Projects are a temporary endeavor undertaken to create a unique product, service, or result.

PMI. PMBOK Guide: A Guide to the Project Management Body of Knowledge. 6th ed. Pennsylvania: PMI, 2017 (adapted).

Based on the project definition presented, evaluate the following statements.

I. The creation of a unique product, or an item of the product, may represent a possible project.  
II. A unique service, or a capability to perform a service, may represent a possible project.  
III. The operation of software, even if repeated many times, may represent a possible project.  
IV. A unique combination of one or more products, services, or results may represent a possible project.

It is correct only what is stated in:

A. I and III.  
B. II and III.  
C. II and IV.  
D. I, II, and IV.  
E. I, III, and IV.
